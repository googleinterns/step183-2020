Scavenger Hunt

Welcome to Scavenger Hunt, a web application that gives travelers a fun way to explore a new city or revisit a familiar one. We will take you on an informative journey around the city by providing you with riddles and hints to find popular landmarks, interesting spots, and hidden gems.

To contribute to our growing collection of destinations, click 'Create a Destination' to add a description, riddle, and hints for a destination to be used in a future scavenger hunt. To go on a new scavenger hunt, click 'Generate a Hunt.' Based on factors such as location, difficulty level, and type of destination, we'll generate a customized scavenger hunt for you.
